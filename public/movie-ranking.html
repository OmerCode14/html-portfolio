The Best Movies According To Omer

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

National Treasure

It’s packed with action, adventure, and historical intrigue, which makes it a fun and engaging watch. The concept of treasure hunts tied to real historical events is fascinating, especially when they tie in famous symbols like the Declaration of Independence and secret codes. Nicolas Cage's performance adds to the film’s charm, bringing energy and a bit of quirky humor to the character of Ben Gates. Plus, the mystery and puzzles keep viewers on the edge of their seats, trying to piece everything together.

Shooter

Mark Wahlberg’s portrayal of Bob Lee Swagger, a sniper with a tortured past, brings a lot of intensity and grit to the role. The film's plot, which weaves in themes of betrayal, conspiracy, and high-stakes action, keeps you hooked from start to finish. There’s also something satisfying about the way Swagger outsmarts his enemies, using his sniper skills and tactical thinking. Plus, the movie has a great mix of explosive action and cerebral moments that make you think.

Avengers: Endgame

It brings together over a decade of storytelling and character arcs, making the payoff for fans really emotional and satisfying. There’s something incredible about seeing so many beloved characters team up for one last mission to save the universe, especially when you get those iconic moments like Captain America wielding Thor’s hammer and the final battle. The movie blends action with heartfelt moments, and it wraps up storylines in such a meaningful way that it feels like a true culmination of the entire MCU.

It's also packed with moments that hit you right in the feels, like Iron Man’s sacrifice and the bittersweet goodbye to Black Widow. The way it balances humor, intense action, and emotional depth is what makes it stand out.
